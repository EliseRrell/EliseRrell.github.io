Dizparc

March-May 2025

Background

The project originated from a need to design a platform for managing procurement projects, as existing solutions slowed down an already lengthy and resource-intensive process.

My Role

Responsibilities were shared among the team, and we employed various design methods to guide our decisions. I take particular pride in contributing to the interface’s color scheme and layout, as well as designing the logo (confidential).

Challenges

  1. Create an engaging and intuitive interface that conveys a calm colour choice while maintaining a unique appearance.
  2. Streamline document submission and approval processes.
  3. Limited access to respondents due to confidentiality.

Team structure

In a team of four, we collaborated to design the webpage, conduct user research, create wireframes and prototypes, and deliver a UX-focused solution for the client.
